About MGKDB

The Multiscale GyroKinetic DataBase (MGKDB) is a cross-platform document-oriented database developed by the Partnership for Multiscale Gyrokinetic Turbulence: A U.S. Dept. of Energy Scientific Discovery Through Advanced Computing (SciDAC) Project. The goal of MGKDB is to create a support framework, interface, and online storage infrastructure for the many computational plasma physics simulation studies aimed at understanding the behavior of plasma in magnetically confined fusion devices. MGKDB is a community tool aimed towards enabling deep data analytics on hidden content (i.e. the latent space) existing within the substantial simulation studies that have already been performed, and will be performed in the future, using capabilities such as: machine learning, artificial intelligence, and data mining. MGKDB is also designed to facilitate validation, verification, and cross-code benchmarking studies, with a strong emphasis on preserving compatibility with all standards and databases already existing within the community.
