About MGKDB

The Multiscale GyroKinetic DataBase (MGKDB) is a cross-platform document-oriented database program developed by the Partnership for Multiscale Gyrokinetic Turbulence: A U.S. Dept. of Energy Scientific Discovery Through Advanced Computing (SciDAC) Project. The goal of MGKDB is to create a support framework, interface, and online storage infrastructure for the many simulation studies performed in computational physics and aimed at understanding the scientific behaviors of plasma in magnetically confined fusion plasmas. MGKDB is a community tool designed to improve the ease with which validation, verification, and cross-code benchmarking studies can be performed. MGKDB is further aimed towards enabling deep data analytics on hidden content (i.e. the latent space) existing within the substantial simulation studies that have already been performed, and will be performed in the future, using capabilities such as: machine learning, artificial intelligence, and data mining.
